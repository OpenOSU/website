> About OpenOSU

Open OSU!

We are Chandler Donahey and Will Garrison, and the vision for our campaign is rooted in the principles of transparency, accessibility, and support for every student at OSU. We are committed to truly accessible information, the expansion of student media, and unwavering support for student-run organizations.

Expanding Student Media

A thriving university requires a well-informed student body, and we believe that a robust media is central to achieving this goal. By allocating time and resources to the Orange Media Network, we will ensure that every major OSU event is reported on.

We also want to create programs focused on the inner workings of the university. Under our administration, the expansion of student media will serve as a catalyst for student engagement and prosperity.

Truly Accessible Information

Our university can be a difficult and intimidating system to navigate. As the leaders of your student government, we will work to ensure that vital information such as financials, staff contacts, and other crucial data are easily available.

Through the implementation of user-friendly platforms and regular updates, we aim to make information more than just available; it should be understandable and empower you to engage meaningfully in the decisions that shape our university.

Supporting Student Organizations

From building our public image to fostering a passionate community, clubs are one of the most impactful aspects of OSU. As club leaders ourselves, we know the obstacles that student organizations face on a daily basis

Our priorities lay in streamlining common processes, making leadership transitions easier, and finding dedicated spaces for clubs. Your efforts and commitment should be matched with the resources and support of the university as we work toward our collective mission of building a stronger OSU community.
